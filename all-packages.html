Payment Plans

Terms & Conditions

a. Full Payment: Full Installation (solar & inverter installation) is necessary for a simple home or office setup and takes a week to completely setup. It comes with a warranty of 1 year and of course, quality is assured.

b. Half Payment: When 50% payment is made, a full Installation (solar & inverter installation) is carried out as long as an agreement is signed (with a blank cheque as collateral) that the balance is to be paid installmentally (split equally within 6 months). It comes with a warranty of 1 year and of course, quality is assured.

c. Installment Payment: Payment will initially be made installmentally until half of the total cost is realized. Then a full Installation (solar & inverter installation) is carried out as long as an agreement is signed (with a blank cheque as a collateral) that the balance is to be paid installmentally within 6 months. It comes with a warranty of 1 year and of course, quality is assured.

NOTE: If any of the installment plan is defaulted, a 5% increment of the defaulted installment will be added to the next month's installment.
