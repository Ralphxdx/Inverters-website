About Us

Solar Edge established in 1998, with the headquarter in Lekki Lagos, which is a professional hi-tech enterprise specialized in Power Inverter, Solar Power, VRLA Battery development, manufacturing, sales and after-service.

Our comprehensive product lines include: Power inverter, Solar inverter, Solar charge controller, Solar battery and some other related Solar products.Our products have passed SAA,CE, SONCAP certificate and IS9001 quality certificate. We are one of the authorized suppliers for Nigerian Government.

In just a few years, SOLAR EDGE solar inverters and components have become synonymous with technological innovation, build quality and unparalleled customer service and are highly spoken of by our customers worldwide.
